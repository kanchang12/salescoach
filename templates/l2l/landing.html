Most AI tools just answer questions. Max runs structured coaching sessions — teach, practise, solo attempt, debrief — and remembers what you struggled with last time.

Before any scenarios, Max asks about your sector, your current role, and your biggest challenge right now. Every session is calibrated to where you actually are.

Not slides. Not theory. Max explains the technique with a scenario from your sector, then puts you in the hot seat. You practice, Max gives feedback, you go again.
