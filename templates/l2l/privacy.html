Privacy policy

What we store about your child

First name and year group only. No surname, no date of birth, no email address, no school name, no photo. That is all.

Who owns the data

You, the parent, are the data controller for your child's information. We are the data processor. We act only on your instructions.

UK GDPR and ICO Children's Code

We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). We comply fully with UK GDPR and the ICO Children's Code (Age Appropriate Design Code). Accounts for children require parental consent at registration.

Your rights

You can request full deletion of all data at any time from your account settings. We will action this within 30 days. No questions asked.

What we never do

We do not sell data. We do not share data with third parties except payment processing (Stripe) and our hosting provider. We do not serve advertising.
