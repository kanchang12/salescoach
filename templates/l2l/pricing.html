Questions

What is the difference between Pro and Essentials?
Only the dashboard access frequency. Pro managers can check in daily and track multiple learners. Essentials is for individual self-directed learners who want a weekly summary. The coaching session itself is identical on both plans.
What sectors does Max cover?
Tech / SaaS, pharma, financial services, construction, recruitment, retail, and general B2B. Max adapts all scenarios and examples to your sector so the practice feels realistic.
What experience levels does it cover?
Junior (0–2 years), mid-level (2–5 years), and senior (5+ years / team lead). Max calibrates difficulty and depth automatically based on your level and session history.
Can I use this for a whole sales team?
Yes. The Pro plan supports multiple learner profiles under one account. Each learner has their own session history, XP, and progress track. The manager dashboard shows all of them in one view.
What data do you store?
First name, sector, experience level, and session history. No call recordings. No CRM data. No data sold to third parties. UK GDPR compliant. You can request full deletion at any time.
Can I cancel?
Yes. Any time. No notice period. No questions asked.
